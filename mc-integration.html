Meteochan Minecraft-Twitch Integration

Here you can find a list of mobs that can be spawned in Minecraft on meteochans stream.
Mobs can be spawned with Bits, Donations or a Subscription.
Only ONE mob will spawn at a time.

10 Bits
Summon a silverfish.

Summon a Ghoul (graveyard:ghoul) or a Zombie.
100 Bits
Summon a Witch or a Hoglin.

  • Alien Zombie (beyond_earth:alien_zombie)
  • Martian Raptor (beyond_earth:martian_raptor)
  • Acolyte (graveyard:acolyte)
  • Hoglin
  • Ghast
300 Bits

  • Cave Creeper (creeperoverhaul:cave_creeper)
  • Jungle Creeper (creeperoverhaul:jungle_creeper)
  • Creeper (minecraft:creeper)
